Level: Beginner / Intermediate
Coach: Nicole
Duration: 20 min
A beginner to moderate express Pilates class perfect for when you are short on time but want to move and feel energised. Release tension and improve flexibility with calm, flowing stretches and controlled movements.

Exercises

Forearm Donkey Kick

1 minute each side

Lift your heel toward the ceiling from a forearm plank position, squeezing the glutes at the top. Targets glutes, hamstrings, and core stability.

Bicycle Crunch

1 minute

Bring one knee toward your chest while rotating the opposite elbow to meet it, extending the other leg straight. Targets abs, obliques, and hip flexors.

Tricep Push-Ups & Shoulder Taps

1 minute

Perform narrow push-ups with elbows grazing the ribcage, then hold a high plank and tap opposite shoulders. Targets triceps, deep core, and obliques.
