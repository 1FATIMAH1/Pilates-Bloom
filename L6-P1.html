Lesson 6 – Intermediate Pilates Flow

Coach: Izzy & Nicole
Level: Intermediate
Duration: 30–50 minutes
Category: Core & Flexibility

Welcome to Lesson 6! In this intermediate-level flow, you will build on the foundation from earlier lessons. This session focuses on core control, spinal mobility, and connecting your breathing with fluid Pilates movements.

Exercise Breakdown

Exercise Duration Target Area Difficulty
Pelvic Tilt 30 seconds Lower back & core Intermediate
Shoulder Bridge 40 seconds Glutes & core stability Intermediate
Swan Stretch 30 seconds Lower back & spine Intermediate
Single Leg Crunch 40 seconds Core & abdominals Intermediate
Arm Raises with Breathing 1 minute Shoulder mobility Intermediate
Tabletop Position 1 minute Core stability & posture Intermediate
Leg Slides 45 seconds Core control Intermediate

What You Will Learn

  • Core Strength & Stability
    • Engage deep abdominal muscles during controlled movements
    • Build core endurance through sustained holds
      • Tabletop position for stability
      • Pelvic tilt for lower back support
  • Spine Flexibility & Mobility
    • Improve spinal extension through Swan Stretch
    • Release stiffness with fluid movement patterns
  • Breathing & Movement Coordination
    • Synchronize breath with each exercise phase
    • Use lateral breathing to maintain core engagement
      • Inhale to prepare
      • Exhale to activate
