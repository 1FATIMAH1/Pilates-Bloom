Level: Beginner / Intermediate
Coach: Nicole
Duration: 28 min
Improve posture, spinal mobility, and core stability through a gentle yet effective Pilates flow. This lesson focuses on back strength, spinal health, and controlled stretching to help release tension and support better body alignment.

Exercises

Bird-Dog with Arm Sweeps

47 seconds

Strengthens the back and deep core by extending the opposite arm and leg with control, improving spinal stability, balance, and coordination.

Bridging with Arm Reach

1 minute 18 seconds

Lifts the hips into a bridge while adding an arm reach to activate the glutes, engage the core, and support pelvic stability.

Prone Back Extension

30 seconds

Gently lifts the chest from the mat to strengthen the lower back, improve posture, and build upper body support.
