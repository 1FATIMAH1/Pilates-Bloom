Level: Beginner / Intermediate
Coach: Izzy
Duration: 20 min
Release tension and improve flexibility with calm, flowing stretches. This lesson helps you build body awareness and improve mobility through controlled, mindful movements.

Exercises

Single/Double Limb Reaches

1 minute

Strengthens the core and improves coordination by reaching opposite arm and leg while maintaining a stable spine.

Side Plank with Thread

1 minute each side

Builds lateral core strength and shoulder stability while improving balance and spinal alignment.

Plank to Pike

1 minute

Engages the full body by transitioning from a plank to a pike position, strengthening the core, shoulders, and hamstrings.
