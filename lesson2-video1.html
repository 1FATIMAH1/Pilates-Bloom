Level: Beginner
Coach: Nicole
Duration: 30 min
This full body Pilates lesson focuses on improving core strength, body alignment, and movement control through slow and controlled exercises. It is suitable for beginners and helps build strength, balance, and stability without any equipment.

Exercises

Toe Taps

30 seconds – 10 repetitions

Strengthens the lower abdominal muscles while improving core stability.

Side Leg Lift

40 seconds – 10 repetitions

Targets the outer thighs and hips while improving stability.

Superman Lift

40 seconds – 8 repetitions

Strengthens the lower back and improves posture.
