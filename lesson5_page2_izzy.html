Lesson 5 – Upper Body Pilates

Coach: Izzy – Upper Body Pilates
Level: Intermediate
Duration: 30 min
A 30-minute upper body and core Pilates workout using resistance bands. This session targets the shoulders, arms, and core through flowing sequences that build strength, improve balance, and develop upper body endurance with controlled Pilates movements.

Lesson Benefits:

⭐ Upper Body Strength with Bands
  • Building arm and shoulder strength using resistance bands
  • Performing curls and presses with proper Pilates form
⭐ Core & Arm Integration
  • Combining core engagement with arm resistance
  • Improving coordination and control
⭐ Plank Stability
  • Progressing from knee hovers to full plank
  • Building endurance and strength
