Level: Beginner / Intermediate
Coach: Nicole
Duration: 28 min
This Pilates lesson focuses on spinal mobility, back strength, and gentle core control. It helps improve posture, release tension, and support spinal health through slow, controlled movement.
Table: Beginner / Intermediate Pilates Exercises
Exercise Duration Target Area Difficulty
Swan Stretch 30 seconds Back & spine flexibility Intermediate
Single Leg Crunch 40 seconds Core & abdominal muscles Intermediate
Tabletop Position 1 minute Core stability & posture Intermediate
Lesson Benefits:
⭐ Improved Spine Mobility
  • Supports gentle extension and flexibility through Swan Stretch
  • Helps release stiffness in the back and improve posture
⭐ Stronger Core Engagement
  • Activates deep abdominal muscles during Single Leg Crunch
  • Builds better control and support for everyday movement
⭐ Better Stability and Balance
  • Improves coordination and body alignment in Tabletop Position
  • Encourages controlled, steady, and balanced movement
