Level: Beginner / Intermediate
Coach: Izzy
Duration: 20 min
This 20-minute ab workout targets the upper and lower abdominals and obliques without any equipment. The lesson emphasizes breath control, maintaining an imprinted spine, and moving with slow, intentional control to maximize engagement.
Table: Beginner / Intermediate Pilates Exercises
Exercise Duration Target Area Difficulty
Tabletop Leg Extensions 1 minute Lower abs & core stability Beginner
Single/Double Limb Reaches 1 minute Core & coordination Beginner / Intermediate
Chest Lifts with Knee Hugs 1 minute Upper abs & flexibility Beginner
Side Planks with Threads 1 minute each side Obliques & shoulder stability Intermediate
Plank-to-Pike Movements 1 minute Core, shoulders & hamstrings Intermediate
Plank Leg Lifts 1 minute Glutes & core control Intermediate
Lesson Benefits:
⭐ Improved Breathing Control
  • Deepens inhales and lengthens exhales for better breath control
  • Helps coordinate breathing with movement for stronger engagement
⭐ Stronger Core Activation
  • Maintains an imprinted spine by tucking the tailbone to engage the core
  • Activates deep abdominal muscles during every exercise
⭐ Better Movement Control
  • Encourages super slow and intentional movement for maximum benefit
  • Builds focus, precision, and stronger control in every exercise
⭐ Greater Full Body Stability
  • Builds shoulder and lateral core strength through side planks
  • Improves balance and body awareness through plank variations
