Home > Training Program > Lesson 5 > Coach Izzy
Level: Intermediate
Coach: Izzy
Duration: 30 min
Build upper body and core strength through a 30-minute Pilates workout using resistance bands. This lesson targets the shoulders, arms, and deep core with controlled movements that improve stability, posture, and muscular endurance.

Exercises

Cat-Cow

Warm-Up

Moving through spinal extension and flexion in a four-point position to mobilize the spine and prepare the body for upper body work.

Chest Lifts with Band Tension

Core & Arms

Lying on the back with the band looped over one leg and held by the opposite hand to activate the bicep during crunches.

Band-Assisted Tricep Push-Ups

Upper Body

Using the band around the elbows to assist with the upward phase of the push-up, targeting triceps and chest while building arm strength.
