Lesson 2 – Core Strength Basics

Coach: Izzy – Core Strength Basics
Level: Beginner
Duration: 15–20 min
This Pilates lesson focuses on strengthening the glutes and core muscles while improving balance and body stability. The exercises help activate the lower body and support spinal alignment through slow and controlled movements.
Table: Booty and Core Pilates Exercises
Exercise Duration Target Area Difficulty
Single Leg Glute Bridge 40 seconds Glutes & Core Beginner – Intermediate
Glute Bridge 40 seconds Glutes & Hamstrings Beginner
Arm Raises with Breathing 1 minute Shoulders & Breathing Control Beginner

Lesson Benefits:

⭐ Glute Strength
  • Strengthening the glute muscles
  • Improving lower body stability
⭐ Core Stability
  • Activating the abdominal muscles
  • Maintaining balance during movement
⭐ Movement Control
  • Performing controlled Pilates movements
  • Improving coordination and posture
