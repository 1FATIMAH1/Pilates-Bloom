Level: Beginner / Intermediate
Coach: Nicole
Duration: 28 min
This Pilates lesson focuses on spinal mobility, back strength, and gentle core control. It helps improve posture, release tension, and support spinal health through slow, controlled movement.
Table: Beginner / Intermediate Pilates Exercises
Exercise Duration Target Area Difficulty
Seated Side Stretch 1 minute 30 seconds Spine mobility & side body Beginner
Cat-Cow Stretch 40 seconds Spinal mobility Beginner
Bird-Dog with Arm Sweeps 47 seconds Back & core stability Beginner / Intermediate
Bridging with Arm Reach 1 minute 18 seconds Glutes & core Beginner / Intermediate
Prone Back Extension 30 seconds Lower back & posture Intermediate
Child’s Pose 18 seconds Back release & recovery Beginner
⭐ What You Will Learn in This Lesson
⭐ Spinal Mobility
  • Improving flexibility and controlled movement through Cat-Cow and side stretches
  • Supporting better posture by moving the spine safely and smoothly
⭐ Back Strength
  • Strengthening the back muscles through Bird-Dog and Prone Back Extension
  • Building stability to support everyday posture and reduce tension
⭐ Core & Glute Control
  • Activating the deep core and glutes during Bridging with Arm Reach
  • Improving body alignment and balance through controlled movements
⭐ Recovery & Stretching
  • Releasing tension in the lower back with Child’s Pose and gentle stretches
  • Learning how to cool down safely after core and back exercises
