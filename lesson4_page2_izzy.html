Level: Beginner / Intermediate
Coach: Izzy
Duration: 20 min
A 20-minute Pilates ab workout designed to work the upper and lower abdominals through flowing sequences. The workout focuses on building strength and endurance by keeping the core engaged and the lower spine imprinted into the mat throughout every movement.
Table: Beginner / Intermediate Pilates Exercises
Exercise Duration Target Area Difficulty
Spine Twists 35 seconds Spine mobility & obliques Beginner
Chest Lifts 45 seconds Upper abdominals & deep core Beginner
Chest Lifts with Tabletop Marches 1 minute 29 seconds Core & hip flexors Beginner / Intermediate
Chest Lifts with Toe Taps 49 seconds Lower abs & core control Beginner / Intermediate
Arm Reaches with Chest Lift 34 seconds Upper abdominals & coordination Intermediate
Cross-Body Elbow-to-Knee Rotation 2 minutes 20 seconds Obliques & core rotation Intermediate
Leg Extensions – Oblique Work 1 minute Obliques & lower abdominals Intermediate
Full-Body Extension and Squeeze 2 minutes 23 seconds Full core & total body Intermediate
Lesson Benefits:
⭐ Stronger Upper & Lower Abdominals
  • Works the upper and lower abdominals through flowing Pilates sequences
  • Builds endurance by keeping the core engaged throughout every exercise
⭐ Better Core Stability & Spine Support
  • Keeps the lower spine imprinted into the mat during all movements
  • Develops deep core stability to support posture and balance
⭐ Improved Oblique Activation
  • Activates the obliques through cross-body rotations
  • Strengthens the sides of the core through leg extensions
⭐ More Controlled Movement
  • Encourages slow and intentional movement for maximum benefit
  • Helps coordinate breath with movement throughout the session
