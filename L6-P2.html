Lesson 6 – Intermediate Pilates Flow

Coach: Izzy & Nicole
Level: Intermediate
Duration: 30–50 minutes

In this second part of Lesson 6, we dive deeper into the benefits of each exercise and how to perform them safely. Review the detailed breakdown below and test your knowledge with the quiz.

Detailed Exercise Guide

Exercise Sets Breathing Pattern Common Mistakes
Pelvic Tilt 3 sets Exhale on tilt, inhale to release Arching lower back too much
Shoulder Bridge 3 sets Inhale at bottom, exhale to lift Pushing hips too high
Swan Stretch 2 sets Inhale to extend, exhale to lower Straining the neck
Single Leg Crunch 3 sets per side Exhale on crunch, inhale to lower Pulling on the neck
Tabletop Position 2 sets Steady lateral breathing Letting the back arch

Lesson Benefits

  • Physical Benefits
    • Improved Spine Mobility
      • Supports gentle extension and flexibility through Swan Stretch
      • Helps release stiffness in the back and improve posture
    • Stronger Core Engagement
      • Activates deep abdominal muscles during Single Leg Crunch
      • Builds better control and support for everyday movement
  • Mind-Body Connection
    • Better Stability and Balance
      • Improves coordination and body alignment in Tabletop Position
      • Encourages controlled, steady, and balanced movement
    • Breathing Awareness
      • Develops conscious breathing habits that carry into daily life
      • Reduces stress and improves focus during exercise
  • Safety Tips
    • Always warm up before starting the flow
    • Stop if you feel sharp pain and consult your coach
