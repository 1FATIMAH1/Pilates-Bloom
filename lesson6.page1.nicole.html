Lesson 6 – Intermediate Flow

Coach: Nicole – Intermediate Flow
Level: Intermediate
Duration: 20–30 min
Move through more dynamic Pilates sequences that challenge strength, control, and coordination.

Exercises

Pilates Breathing

1 minute

Improves breathing control and prepares the body for movement.

Glute Bridge

40 seconds

Strengthens glutes and supports lower back stability.

Arm Raises with Breathing

1 minute

Improves shoulder mobility and breathing coordination.
