Level: Beginner
Coach: Nicole
Duration: 10-15 min
This lesson introduces basic Pilates exercises that improve core strength, body alignment, and breathing control. It helps build a strong foundation for beginner students

Exercises

Side Leg Lift

30 seconds - 10 repetitions

Strengthens the outer thighs and hips while improving balance and core stability.

Shoulder Bridge

40 seconds - 8 repetitions

Helps improve core stability and coordination by extending the opposite arm and leg.

Arm Raises with Breathing

1 minute - 10 repetitions

Activates the abdominal muscles and improves control of the lower body during movement.
