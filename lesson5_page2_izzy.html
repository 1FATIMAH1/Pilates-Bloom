Level: Intermediate
Coach: Izzy
Duration: 30 min
A 30-minute upper body and core Pilates workout using resistance bands. This session targets the shoulders, arms, and core through flowing sequences that build strength, improve balance, and develop upper body endurance with controlled Pilates movements.

⭐ What You Will Learn in This Lesson
⭐ Upper Body Strength with Bands
  • Building arm and shoulder strength using resistance bands for controlled tension
  • Performing bicep curls, overhead presses, and tricep push-ups with proper Pilates form
⭐ Core & Arm Integration
  • Combining core engagement with arm resistance to create full upper-body activation
  • Maintaining a chest lift while managing band tension for coordination and control
⭐ Plank Stability & Endurance
  • Progressing from knee hovers to full plank holds and marches
  • Building shoulder and core endurance through sustained plank variations
⭐ Balance & Controlled Movement
  • Using slow, intentional movements to develop balance and body awareness
  • Cooling down with thoracic spine stretches to release shoulder and upper back tension
