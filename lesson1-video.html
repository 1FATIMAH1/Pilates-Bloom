Lesson 1 – Introduction to Pilates

Coach: Izzy – Introduction to Pilates
Level: Beginner
Duration: 10–15 min
This lesson builds the foundation of body awareness and breathing control. It improves posture and prepares the body for advanced movements.

Exercises

Pelvic Tilt

30 seconds - 10 repetitions

Helps activate the core and gently mobilize the lower spine while improving pelvic control.

Shoulder Bridge

40 seconds - 8 repetitions

Strengthens the glutes and hamstrings while supporting spinal stability.

Arm Raises with Breathing

1 minute - 10 repetitions

Improves breathing coordination while gently mobilizing the shoulders.
