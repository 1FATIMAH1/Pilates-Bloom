Level: Beginner
Coach: Izzy
Duration: 15-20 min
This Pilates lesson focuses on strengthening the glutes and abdominal muscles while improving balance and body control. The exercises combine controlled movements and core engagement to support stability and build lower body strength.

Exercises

Single Leg Glute Bridge

40 seconds – 8 repetitions

Strengthens the glutes and core while improving hip stability and balance.

Glute Bridge

40 seconds – 8 repetitions

Strengthens the glutes and hamstrings while supporting spinal stability.

Arm Raises with Breathing

1 minute – 10 repetitions

Improves breathing coordination while gently mobilizing the shoulders.
