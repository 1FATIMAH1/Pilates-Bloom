Level: Intermediate
Coach: Nicole
Duration: 32 min
This full-body Pilates flow by Move With Nicole combines standing leg work, core circuits, side-lying glute isolation, and upper body conditioning into a complete intermediate session. Every major muscle group is targeted through controlled, flowing movements.

⭐ What You Will Learn in This Lesson
⭐ Full Body Strength & Endurance
  • Working every major muscle group in one flowing session for total body conditioning
  • Building muscular endurance through standing, supine, and side-lying circuits
⭐ Standing Leg Work & Balance
  • Developing lower body strength through squats with heel lifts and side-step variations
  • Improving balance and proprioception with controlled weight shifts
⭐ Glute Isolation Techniques
  • Targeting the glutes through side-lying bicycle legs, kicks, and circles
  • Strengthening the hip stabilizers for better pelvic alignment and control
⭐ Core & Upper Body Integration
  • Combining core work with arm conditioning through tricep pumps and push-ups
  • Cooling down with full-body stretches to improve flexibility and recovery
