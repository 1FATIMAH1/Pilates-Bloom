Lesson 6 – Intermediate Flow

Coach: Nicole – Intermediate Flow
Level: Intermediate
Duration: 20–30 min
Table: Beginner Pilates Exercises
Exercise Duration Target Area Difficulty
Pelvic Tilt 30 seconds Lower back & core Beginner
Shoulder Bridge 40 seconds Glutes & core stability Beginner
Arm Raises with Breathing 1 minute Shoulder mobility Beginner
Leg Slides 45 seconds Core control Beginner
Lesson Benefits:
⭐ Improved Breathing Control
  • Develops controlled Pilates breathing techniques
  • Improves coordination between breathing and movement
⭐ Stronger Core and Glutes
  • Strengthens glute muscles through the bridge exercise
  • Improves lower-back stability and core engagement
⭐ Better Body Stability
  • Enhances balance and posture control
  • Encourages slow, controlled and precise movements
