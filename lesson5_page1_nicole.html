Level: Intermediate
Coach: Nicole
Duration: 32 min
A 30-minute full-body Pilates flow targeting every major muscle group. This intermediate workout by Move With Nicole combines standing leg work, core circuits, glute isolation, and arm conditioning into one seamless session to build strength, flexibility, and body awareness.

Exercises

Parallel Squats

Standing Legs

Parallel squats with low pulses followed by side-step squats with arm sweeps to engage the glutes and lower body.

Frog Curl Up

Core & Abs

Knees open with toes together, curling up to deeply activate the lower abdominals and inner thighs simultaneously.

Side-Lying Leg Series

Glute Isolation

Bicycle legs, front-to-back kicks, and leg circles while lying on the side to isolate and strengthen the glutes and hips.
