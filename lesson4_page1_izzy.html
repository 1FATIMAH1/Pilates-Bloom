Home > Training Program > Lesson 4 > Coach Izzy
Level: Beginner / Intermediate
Coach: Izzy
Duration: 20 min
Improve posture and balance with controlled stability work. This lesson focuses on upper abdominal strength, oblique engagement, and deep core activation through slow and intentional Pilates movements.

Exercises

Chest Lifts

45 seconds

Targets the upper abdominals and deep core by lifting the chest while keeping the lower back connected to the mat.

Arm Reaches with Chest Lift

34 seconds

Adds arm reaches on top of the chest lift to further intensify upper abdominal engagement and improve coordination.

Leg Extensions – Oblique Work

1 minute

Targets the obliques and lower abdominals while maintaining spine connection to the mat throughout the movement.
