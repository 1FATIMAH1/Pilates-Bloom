Level: Beginner
Coach: Izzy
Duration: 10-15 min
This beginner Pilates lesson introduces the fundamental principles of breathing, core activation, and posture control. The exercises focus on building body awareness, improving spinal alignment, and strengthening the abdominal and lower back muscles through slow and controlled movements.
