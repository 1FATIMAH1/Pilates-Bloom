Level: Beginner / Intermediate
Coach: Nicole
Duration: 20 min
This express Pilates class combines glute work, core training, and upper body strength into one efficient session. Perfect for building total body strength while improving flexibility and body awareness.
Table: Beginner / Intermediate Pilates Exercises
Exercise Duration Target Area Difficulty
Forearm Donkey Kick 1 minute each side Glutes & hamstrings Beginner
Bicycle Crunch 1 minute Abs & obliques Beginner / Intermediate
Tricep Push-Ups 1 minute Triceps & shoulders Intermediate
Shoulder Taps 1 minute Core & obliques Intermediate
⭐ What You Will Learn in This Lesson
⭐ Glute Activation
  • Isolating and strengthening the glutes through Forearm Donkey Kicks
  • Maintaining core stability while performing lower body movements
⭐ Core & Oblique Strength
  • Training the abs and obliques through Bicycle Crunches
  • Building rotational strength for better posture and balance
⭐ Upper Body Control
  • Strengthening the triceps with narrow push-ups
  • Improving shoulder stability through controlled Shoulder Taps
⭐ Full Body Stability
  • Training the core to resist rotation during Shoulder Taps
  • Connecting breath with movement throughout the session
